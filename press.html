01 Official Bio

Who Is
Bröki.

Bröki is something different. Started in an apartment far away and travelled through space and time just to bring you something new.

Formed in Budapest, Bröki explores the quiet spaces between genres — crafting soundscapes that shimmer with raw energy and quiet intention. Their sound blends hazy textures with direct impact, creating a world that feels both urgent and surreal.

This isn't background music. This is for the moment right before everything changes — built on chaos, shaped by intention.
