official bio

broeki drifts between dream‑pop, soft electronics, and pastel‑washed melancholy. Their sound blends hazy textures with intimate vocals, creating a world that feels both fragile and surreal — like a memory you can almost touch.

Formed in Budapest, broeki explores the quiet spaces between genres, crafting soundscapes that shimmer with emotion and soft distortion.
